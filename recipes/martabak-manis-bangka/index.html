Martabak Manis Bangka (Sweet Stuffed Pancake)

★★★★☆ 4.9 (98 reviews)
⏱ Prep: 30m | Cook: 60m | Total: 90m 👥 Servings: 4 loyang 📊 Difficulty: Medium 🍽️ Category: Snacks & Appetizers

Martabak Manis Bangka (Sweet Stuffed Pancake) is a beloved Indonesian dish that brings together the rich flavors of traditional spices and fresh ingredients. This authentic recipe guides you through every step, from selecting the right ingredients to achieving the perfect texture and taste. Whether you're a seasoned cook or trying Indonesian cuisine for the first time, this recipe delivers restaurant-quality results right in your own kitchen.

Martabak Manis Bangka (Sweet Stuffed Pancake) represents the heart of Indonesian home cooking — flavorful, aromatic, and made with love. This step-by-step guide ensures perfect results every time, whether you're cooking for family, friends, or just yourself.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 300 gr tepung terigu
  • 3 butir telur
  • 60 gr gula pasir
  • 400 ml susu cair
  • 1 sdt baking powder
  • ½ sdt soda kue
  • 1 sdt ragi instan
  • Topping: meses, keju, kacang, susu kental manis, margarin

👨‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Campur terigu, gula, garam, ragi, dan susu. Diamkan 45-60 menit.
  2. Tambahkan telur, baking powder, soda kue. Aduk rata.
  3. Panaskan wajan martabak, tuang adonan.
  4. Tunggu gelembung, taburi gula pasir. Tutup, masak hingga matang.
  5. Olesi margarin, taburi topping, lipat, potong-potong.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients for the best flavor.
  • • Adjust seasoning to your taste — traditional Indonesian cooking is all about balance.
  • • Prep all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place) for a smooth workflow.

🔄 Variations

  • Protein swap — substitute the main protein with chicken, tofu, or tempeh
  • Spice level — adjust chili quantity for mild or extra spicy version
  • Dietary — make it vegan by omitting animal products and using plant-based alternatives

🍚 What to Serve With

This dish pairs perfectly with:

  • Steamed jasmine rice or coconut rice (nasi uduk)
  • Kerupuk (Indonesian shrimp crackers) for crunch
  • Sambal terasi or sambal matah for extra heat
  • Fresh cucumber and tomato slices as a refreshing side
  • Try serving with Bakso Malang (Complete Beef Meatball Soup)
