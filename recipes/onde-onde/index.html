Onde-Onde

★★★★☆ 4.6 (92 reviews)
⏱ Prep: 20m | Cook: 40m | Total: 60m 👥 Servings: 20 buah 📊 Difficulty: Medium 🍽️ Category: Snacks & Appetizers

Onde-Onde is a beloved Indonesian dish that brings together the rich flavors of traditional spices and fresh ingredients. This authentic recipe guides you through every step, from selecting the right ingredients to achieving the perfect texture and taste. Whether you're a seasoned cook or trying Indonesian cuisine for the first time, this recipe delivers restaurant-quality results right in your own kitchen.

Onde-Onde represents the heart of Indonesian home cooking — flavorful, aromatic, and made with love. This step-by-step guide ensures perfect results every time, whether you're cooking for family, friends, or just yourself.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 250 gr tepung ketan
  • Kacang hijau kupas
  • 100 gr gula pasir
  • Wijen
  • Minyak

👨‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Kukus kacang hijau, haluskan.
  2. Buat adonan, isi kacang hijau.
  3. Gulingkan di wijen.
  4. Goreng hingga kecoklatan.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients for the best flavor.
  • • Adjust seasoning to your taste — traditional Indonesian cooking is all about balance.
  • • Prep all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place) for a smooth workflow.

🔄 Variations

  • Protein swap — substitute the main protein with chicken, tofu, or tempeh
  • Spice level — adjust chili quantity for mild or extra spicy version
  • Dietary — make it vegan by omitting animal products and using plant-based alternatives

🍚 What to Serve With

This dish pairs perfectly with:

  • Steamed jasmine rice or coconut rice (nasi uduk)
  • Kerupuk (Indonesian shrimp crackers) for crunch
  • Sambal terasi or sambal matah for extra heat
  • Fresh cucumber and tomato slices as a refreshing side
  • Try serving with Klepon

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Onde-Onde?

Onde-Onde is a traditional Indonesian dish made with authentic spices and fresh ingredients. It's known for its rich flavor and cultural significance in Indonesian cuisine.

How long does it take to make Onde-Onde?

The total time is approximately 60 minutes, including preparation and cooking time.

How many servings does this recipe make?

This recipe yields 20 buah.

Can I make this dish ahead of time?

Yes! Many Indonesian dishes actually taste better the next day as the flavors continue to develop. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

What do you serve with Onde-Onde?

This dish pairs beautifully with steamed jasmine rice, kerupuk (Indonesian crackers), and sambal (chili sauce).

Is this dish spicy?

The spice level is adjustable. You can reduce or increase the chili according to your preference.

Can I make this easier or vegetarian?

You can adapt this recipe by substituting protein with tofu or tempeh. Adjust cooking times accordingly.
