Perkedel Kentang (Indonesian Potato Fritters)

★★★★☆ 4.3 (86 reviews)
⏱ Prep: 10m | Cook: 20m | Total: 30m 👥 Servings: 4 orang 📊 Difficulty: Easy 🍽️ Category: Snacks & Appetizers

Perkedel Kentang (Indonesian Potato Fritters) is a beloved Indonesian dish that brings together the rich flavors of traditional spices and fresh ingredients. This authentic recipe guides you through every step, from selecting the right ingredients to achieving the perfect texture and taste. Whether you're a seasoned cook or trying Indonesian cuisine for the first time, this recipe delivers restaurant-quality results right in your own kitchen.

Perkedel Kentang (Indonesian Potato Fritters) represents the heart of Indonesian home cooking — flavorful, aromatic, and made with love. This step-by-step guide ensures perfect results every time, whether you're cooking for family, friends, or just yourself.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 500 gr kentang
  • 2 butir telur
  • 3 siung bawang putih goreng
  • 2 batang daun seledri iris
  • 1 sdt merica
  • 1 sdt garam
  • Minyak untuk menggoreng

👨‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Kupas kentang, potong, goreng hingga empuk.
  2. Haluskan kentang selagi panas.
  3. Campur dengan bawang putih goreng halus, seledri, merica, garam.
  4. Tambahkan 1 butir telur, aduk rata.
  5. Bentuk bulat pipih.
  6. Celupkan ke telur kocok, goreng hingga kecoklatan.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients for the best flavor.
  • • Adjust seasoning to your taste — traditional Indonesian cooking is all about balance.
  • • Prep all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place) for a smooth workflow.

🔄 Variations

  • Protein swap — substitute the main protein with chicken, tofu, or tempeh
  • Spice level — adjust chili quantity for mild or extra spicy version
  • Dietary — make it vegan by omitting animal products and using plant-based alternatives

🍚 What to Serve With

This dish pairs perfectly with:

  • Steamed jasmine rice or coconut rice (nasi uduk)
  • Kerupuk (Indonesian shrimp crackers) for crunch
  • Sambal terasi or sambal matah for extra heat
  • Fresh cucumber and tomato slices as a refreshing side
  • Try serving with Es Campur (Surabaya Mixed Ice Dessert)
